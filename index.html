Standard Operating Procedure

Document Verification & Official Correspondence Process

A structured workflow for handling verification requests, correspondence tracking, approval, dispatch, and official record maintenance.

Explore Workflow Responsibility Matrix

Quick Overview

Core SOP highlights and operational scope.

Purpose

Define standard procedures for document verification, official correspondence, dispatch, and tracking.

Scope

Degree verification, accreditation, fee details, pharmacy council verification, regulatory correspondence.

Departments

CMS, AO Office, CD Office, Authorized Signatory, Dispatch Team.

Timeline

Internal processing within 7 working days and dispatch delivery within 5–7 days.

Workflow / Process Flow

Interactive SOP process timeline.

1

Receipt of Letter

Verification letters are initially received at CMS.

2

Forwarding of Request

CMS forwards to AO Office → CD Office → concerned official.

3

Entry & Record Maintenance

All requests must be entered into division records before processing.

4

Review & Categorization

Requests categorized into standard verification, fee details, pharmacy, accreditation, special cases.

5

Drafting of Reply

Reply/verification letter is prepared after reviewing supporting documents.

6

Reference Number Generation

Generated using Mail Dispatch Entry Interface with required communication details.

7

Approval & Signature

Draft reply is approved and signed by authorized signatory.

8

Sealing & Dispatch Preparation

Signed documents are sealed and prepared for dispatch.

9

Dispatch of Reply

Replies dispatched via Registered Post, Business Post, Email, or By Hand.

10

Uploading & Record Closure

Soft copy uploaded in UMS, dispatch and tracking records updated.
